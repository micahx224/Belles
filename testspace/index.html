Dressed in trademark red and white corsets and top hats, and accompanied with our fool and horse, the Belles of London City have captured the attention of the national press and delighted audiences around the country.

Our entertainment can be tailored for all kinds of events - nightclubs and venues in London's hip East End, stage performances, private parties, weddings, corporate events, festivals and school workshops.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Workshops

As well as our public performances, we also provide traditional dance and music school workshops  which aim to build confidence, co-ordination, curiosity and musical agility.  For Primary school years 1-6.

We also run workshops for children and adults as part of a festival or special event.
"Sacred Heart were lucky enough to have Belles of London City come in and perform for us on St Georges Day! Year 1 and 2 had workshops where they were able to try out the traditional Morris dancing and they had a great time! One of our teachers said it was the best workshop we have had all year! I thoroughly recommend Belles of London City Morris dancing to anyone who wants to enjoy and celebrate traditional English culture! We will definitely be having them back next year!"  
Sacred Heart Primary School
