Private functions
Whether you want short bursts of song and dance or longer performances, we can provide the entertainment you are looking for.  Our sets can be performed in intimate and large venues and we are more than happy to encourage audiences to join in by teaching them a few of our easier dances or bringing everyone together in a group singalong.

Festivals
We have performed at several festivals in our career both on stage and as a pop-up act.  As a group of talented dancers, musicians, stage performers and artists we offer an eclectic range of performances, workshops and experiences based on traditional folk for modern audiences.  We're working on some exciting festival ideas this year!

 School events
We specialise in one-day folk music and dance workshops for all primary year groups, introducing children to a range of traditional dances including morris stick dances, garland dances, line dances and hanky dances, as well as singing, music and crafts.
